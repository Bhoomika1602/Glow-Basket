Stay ahead of the curve with the latest makeup trends. Here's what's hot this season:

1. Glowy Skin

Move over, matte! Radiant, dewy skin is in. Focus on hydrating primers, luminous foundations, and liquid highlighters for that lit-from-within look.

2. Bold Lips

Make a statement with vibrant lip colors. Think fiery reds, deep berries, and electric oranges. A bold lip pairs perfectly with minimal eye makeup.

3. Graphic Eyeliner

Experiment with different eyeliner shapes and colors. Floating crease lines, negative space designs, and colorful wings are popular choices.

4. Fluffy Brows

Natural, brushed-up brows are still trending. Use a brow gel to set your brows in place for a full and feathery look.

Tips for Trying New Trends:

  • Start with one trend at a time to ease into it.
  • Practice makes perfect! Don't be afraid to experiment.
  • Use good quality products for better results and longevity.
  • Watch tutorials online for step-by-step guidance.

Have fun exploring these trends and find what works best for you!
